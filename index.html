Load a face-on or down-the-line video, then hit ✨ Auto-track (AI).

The AI pose-tracks the swing in your browser, auto-calibrates from the golfer's height, and tags P1–P10. Then step frame-by-frame or jump to a P and drag any dot to correct it — AI points show a dashed ring until you fix them.

Zoom with the scroll wheel or the +/− buttons to place dots precisely; Pan to move around. Prefer full manual? Calibrate (3 clicks) and place points yourself.
